About Me

I am a Mechanical Engineer with a primary interest in deep-rooted problems – from machine vision development, mechanical design, and FEA/CFD modeling to process development/continuous improvement

I have completed my Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from California State University Fresno (Fresno State

Most of my work and industry experience is in multidisciplinary industries, which have involved:

  • The design of HVAC systems for commercial applications
  • CFD/FEA/DEA simulation and analysis for various applications (i.e., Wear modeling of backhoe buckets, drag coefficient modeling of corn grain, hyper-elastic modeling of non-linear applications, and so on)
  • Manufacturing of Li-ion battery cells for automotive/residential applications
  • Manufacturing of Aluminum castings and CNC machining for automotive applications
  • Manufacturing of light-duty valves, actuators, and DC motors for commercial applications

I have held leadership roles in teams ranging from 2 to 25 student/professional members and have additional experience in teaching undergraduate mechanical engineering students, project management, and solar cell research
